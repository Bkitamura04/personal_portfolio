Meta Social Media Marketing

Throughout the Meta Social Media Marketing Professional Certificate, I gained valuable skills and knowledge that have prepared me for a career in social media marketing. The program covered the fundamentals of social media management, advertising, and optimization across major platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and TikTok.

The course also provided hands-on projects that allowed me to apply what I learned, such as creating a social media brand presence, developing effective posts, and building full ad campaigns using Meta Ads Manager. By the end of the program, I had a project-based portfolio that I could showcase to potential employers or clients.
